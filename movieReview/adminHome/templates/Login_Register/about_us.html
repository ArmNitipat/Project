About Us

This project is created for educational purposes and does not seek profit.

We are a Team have only 2 people who crafted this website as part of our project, currently in our sophomore year at Mahanakorn University of Technology.

Our platform uses AI to analyze the sentiments of movies. To know if the movie is positive or negative.

Opinion information from other websites has been collected and displayed on our website for convenient access to a variety of information at the same time.

We hope you enjoy our website and find it useful.

Objective

We want to provide a platform for movie enthusiasts to share their thoughts and opinions on their favorite movies and actors. We aim to create a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for cinema.

Vision

We want to create a platform that is accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or experience. We want to provide a space where people can come together to discuss and celebrate their love of movies.

Contact us

If you have any questions or feedback, please feel free to contact us. We would love to hear from you!

Our Team

Our team is made up of a group of passionate movie enthusiasts who are dedicated to creating a platform that is inclusive and welcoming to all.
